Time Recording - Breaks


Putting in breaks

Option 1: Click "Check out now" when break starts, "Check in now" when break ends

Option 2: For pre defined break times use one of the <<Templates>>.
Note you can create your own template entries - just tap the "Edit" icon top right on the Template screen.

Option 3, for advanced users: If your break times are pretty much static then have a look at <<Automatic breaks>> on Preferences, page 2, at the bottom. This allows to define default break time and length based on work time total.

Reporting break times
• Go to Menu / <<Reports>>
• Tap the E3 detail settings icon and tick the field "Break"
• Click the E3 entry to start the report
Also see the Reports page for more information
