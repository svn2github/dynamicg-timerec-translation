Time Recording: Backup & Restore

(1) Backup to Google Drive or Dropbox
• "Create backup" is a one time backup of all your app data and settings to your Cloud Provider (Google Drive or Dropbox)
• We strongly recommend you enable "Auto backup" if you regularly use Time Recording (green box in picture 2) - if your phone gets lost or stolen, having a recent "Cloud" backup is the only way to recover your data.
• At this time no other Cloud providers are supported (i.e. no support for OwnCloud, Box, OneDrive or others)
• For historic reason, the app requires full read/write access to your Dropbox. If this is a concern to you then use Google Drive instead, where the app has "own files only" access.


(2) Restore
• Restore will download the lastest backup file from your Cloud account, then copy over all data and settings from that backup file into the app. Note all previously existing data within the app is overwritten.
